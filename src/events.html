Happy Hour - Every Wednesday at 6-7pm EST

Join via Zoom

Attention all aspiring and current web/software developers! Join me in a virtual meeting for a free mentoring session on a topic of your choice. First come first serve!

Get help with a project, talk programming topics, practice interviewing, get career advice, or anything you want!

Each participant gets 20 minutes (or more, if no one is in the queue) while spectators can take notes or chat. All sessions are recorded for my personal use.

Free to join, all welcome, at every level of expertise.
